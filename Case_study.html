CASE STUDY

Helping an innovative compliance company assist their AWS clients to remain compliant at lower cost.

About the Client

SequenceShift provides innovative, next level compliance solutions for AWS customers that solve digital transformation problems. ​The SequenceShift mission is to enable Amazon Connect customers to quickly and securely accept payments in their contact centres by offering fully PCI DSS compliant cloud based services that can be accessed without setup fees and ongoing commitments.

How We Helped

SequenceShift has been able to reduce the time and effort required for Amazon Connect customers to implement processing of card payments in their contact centre. This has allowed AWS customers to maintain PCI DSS compliance at a lower cost than traditional on-premise environments via migration of contact centre card payment processes to Amazon Connect.
